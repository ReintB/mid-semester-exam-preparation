## Vocabulary

1. **Ahmet** *should have ____ (practiced)* before speaking in front of the class.
A. brought his business cards
**B. practiced his presentation**
C. worn jeans
D. gotten gas for the car
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Melakukan latihan presentasi adalah hal paling masuk akal yang disarankan sebelum berbicara di depan umum.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Ahmet** | Verb: *should have practiced*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Modal - Advice):** Subject + should have + Verb 3

2. Dave, **this** *is* the package that *arrived* for you this morning _____.
A. documentary
B. with a free case
C. on sale
**D. by courier**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Paket (package) yang tiba di pagi hari biasanya dikirimkan melalui jasa kurir (by courier).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **this**, **package (that)** | Verb: *is*, *arrived*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Past with Relative Clause):** Subject + Verb 2

3. **We** *bought* tickets for the show at the last minute. **We** *were* lucky **it** *hadn't ______ (sold out)*!
A. stopped
B. made reservations
**C. sold out**
D. attended
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Sold out" adalah istilah lazim untuk tiket atau barang yang sudah habis terjual.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **We**, **it (tickets)** | Verb: *bought*, *were*, *hadn't sold out*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Perfect Negative):** Subject + had + not + Verb 3

4. **I** *should have ____ (gotten)* gas for the car before our long drive.
A. ordered an extra large coffee
**B. gotten gas**
C. left my bag
D. brought his business cards
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Gotten gas" (secara harfiah berarti mengisi bensin) adalah persiapan krusial untuk mobil sebelum melakukan perjalanan jauh.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **I** | Verb: *should have gotten*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Modal - Penyesalan masa lalu):** Subject + should have + Verb 3

5. The lucky **man** that *won* the ____ *quit* his job and *retired* early.
**A. lottery**
B. game
C. sale
D. competition
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menang lotre (lottery) dalam jumlah besar umumnya memungkinkan seseorang untuk membiayai sisa hidupnya, berhenti kerja, dan pensiun dini.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **man**, **that (relative pronoun)** | Verb: *won*, *quit*, *retired*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Past - Serangkaian aksi):** Subject + Verb 2 and Verb 2

6. The **____ (cookies)** that **you** *made* *are* delicious.
A. village
B. anniversary
C. coffee
**D. cookies**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kata "delicious" merujuk pada rasa makanan dan "made" merujuk pada dibuat/dipanggang. Pilihan paling tepat adalah kue kering (cookies).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **cookies**, **you** | Verb: *are*, *made*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Tense with Relative Clause):** Noun + that + Subject + Verb 1 + is/are/am + Adjective

7. By the end of June, **Arup** *had attended* six ______ for his friends and relatives who finished college.
**A. graduation parties**
B. engagement parties
C. museum exhibits
D. competitions
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Perayaan untuk orang yang menyelesaikan kuliah (finished college) disebut pesta kelulusan (graduation parties).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Arup** | Verb: *had attended*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Perfect Tense):** Subject + had + Verb 3

8. Ocean **levels** *are rising* because **____ (glaciers)** *are melting*.
A. habitats
B. climates
C. solar powers
**D. glaciers**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Mencairnya bongkahan es di kutub atau pegunungan tinggi (glaciers) secara fisik menyebabkan kenaikan volume dan permukaan air laut.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **levels**, **glaciers** | Verb: *are rising*, *are melting*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Continuous - Sebab Akibat):** Subject + is/am/are + Verb-ing + because + Subject + is/am/are + Verb-ing

9. Since their **____ (habitats)** *are disappearing*, **tigers** *are* endangered.
**A. habitats**
B. animals
C. ocean levels
D. metropolitans
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Hilangnya tempat tinggal alami atau habitat liar membuat hewan berada dalam status terancam punah (endangered).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **habitats**, **tigers** | Verb: *are disappearing*, *are (endangered di sini sbg adj)*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Continuous & Present Nominal):** Since + Subject + is/am/are + Verb-ing, Subject + is/am/are + Adjective

10. That*'s* the Peruvian **restaurant** that **we** *went* to for our wedding _____.
A. conference
B. propose
**C. anniversary**
D. resort
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Merayakan momen peringatan tahunan (dalam hal ini hari jadi pernikahan / wedding anniversary) tepat dilakukan secara intim di sebuah restoran khusus.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **That**, **we** | Verb: *is ('s)*, *went*
> **Rumus / Pola (Relative Clause di Masa Lampau):** Noun + that + Subject + Verb 2

## Grammar

1. Which cell **phone** *comes* with a free case and **____ (which)** *does* not?
A. that
**B. which**
C. whose
D. those
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Which" digunakan sebagai kata ganti referensi (Relative Pronoun) untuk merujuk atau menanyakan pilihan benda yang spesifik. Di sini merujuk pada HP mana yang dapat gratis casing dan mana (HP) yang tidak.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **phone**, **which** | Verb: *comes*, *does not (come)*
> **Rumus / Pola (Interrogative Pronoun for Choices):** Which + Noun + / Which + Auxiliary Verb

2. There *are* more **floods** _____ there *are* more typhoons and hurricanes.
**A. because**
B. so
C. , because
D. , since
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menghubungkan akibat (banjir) dengan alasannya (topan badai). Di tengah klausa, "because" tidak memerlukan koma sebelum kata tersebut.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **floods**, **typhoons and hurricanes** | Verb: *are*, *are*
> **Rumus / Pola (Conjunction of Cause):** Result Clause + because + Reason Clause

3. Remember in Senegal there *was* ____ small **village** where **goats** *walked* in the streets?
A. who
B. where
**C. that**
D. which
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Dalam kalimat ini ada komponen "Remember ... there was a small village" yang diartikan "masih ingat desa kecil itu...". Di opsi "that" merupakan demonstrative yang menunjukkan benda spesifik ("itu/that small village").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **village**, **goats** | Verb: *was*, *walked*
> **Rumus / Pola (Demonstrative Adjective):** that + singular noun

4. *Are* **you** still tired? **You** *____ (should have)* ordered an extra large coffee.
A. shouldn't have
**B. should have**
C. may not have
D. may have
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kalimat ini menyatakan saran terhadap aksi yang sebenarnya sudah lewat/tidak dilakukan. Jika lelah sekarang, "seharusnya tadi kamu pesan kopi ukuran besar".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you** | Verb: *Are*, *should have ordered*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Modals - Unfulfilled Advice):** Subject + should have + Verb 3

5. Make sure to go to the **bakery** **____ (that)** *sells* delicious pastries.
**A. that**
B. by that
C. who
D. where
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "That" berfungsi sebagai subjek klausa relatif (Relative Pronoun) yang merujuk pada benda (bakery). Jika menggunakan "where" tidak boleh langsung menyambung kata kerja (sells).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: (Implied You), **bakery/that** | Verb: *Make sure (Imperative)*, *sells*
> **Rumus / Pola (Relative Pronoun - Subject of Clause):** Noun (thing) + that/which + Verb

6. On Monday my **boss** *realized* that **someone** *____ (had left)* all the lights on at work over the weekend.
**A. had left**
B. has left
C. had leave
D. have leave
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Terdapat dua kejadian lampau. "Meninggalkan lampu" terjadi sebelum bos "menyadarinya" di hari senin. Aksi yang lebih lampau menggunakan Past Perfect Tense.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **boss**, **someone** | Verb: *realized*, *had left*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Perfect combined with Simple Past):** Subject + Verb 2 + that + Subject + had + Verb 3

7. That *was* the **conference** **____ (where)** my **professor and I** *gave* a presentation on our lab's research.
A. with
B. that
C. who
**D. where**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Konferensi adalah acara atau tempat kiasan di mana (where) suatu kejadian dilakukan (presentasi). 
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **That**, **professor and I** | Verb: *was*, *gave*
> **Rumus / Pola (Relative Adverb - Location):** Place/Event + where + Subject + Verb

8. *Did* **you** *need* a ride? **We** *____ (could have)* picked you up at the airport.
A. might not have
B. may not have
**C. could have**
D. shouldn't have
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menunjukkan kemampuan di masa lampau yang tidak dilaksanakan. "Kami bisa saja menjemputmu tadi, kalau kamu butuh tumpangan."
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you**, **We** | Verb: *Did need*, *could have picked*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Modals - Unfulfilled Ability):** Subject + could have + Verb 3

9. Their **habitat** *is disappearing*, **____ (so)** polar **bears** *are endangered*.
**A. so**
B. because
C. where
D. since
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Format kebalikan dari soal No 2. Ini adalah kalimat sebab duluan (habitat hilang), dipisahkan koma, lalu diikuti hasil/akibat sehingga dihubungkan dengan "so" (jadi).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **habitat**, **bears** | Verb: *is disappearing*, *are*
> **Rumus / Pola (Conjunction of Result):** Reason Clause, + so + Result Clause

10. My **friends** *____ (had)* brunch at that restaurant last week. The specialty is omeletes.
A. have brunch
**B. had brunch**
C. hadn't brunch
D. eaten brunch
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Keberadaan signal waktu spesifik "last week" menunjukkan aksi selesai di masa lalu murni, sehingga menggunakan Verb 2 dari "have" yaitu "had".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **friends** | Verb: *had*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Past Tense):** Subject + Verb 2

## Language and Use

1. *Had* **they** *made* a reservation before they arrived at the restaurant?
A. It's a new restaurant.
**B. No, they hadn't.**
C. Yes, they went to brunch.
D. No, they did.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Jawaban singkat negatif ini menyesuaikan langsung dengan tata bahasa di awal kalimat, yaitu Auxiliary "Had" (Past Perfect). Short respon-nya harus Yes/No diikuti pronoun dan Auxiliary tersebut.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **they** | Verb: *Had made*, *hadn't*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Past Perfect):** Yes, Subject + had. / No, Subject + hadn't.

2. Where *is* **Toby**? **He** *should be* here by now.
**A. He could have gotten lost on his way here.**
B. He should have gotten lost on his way here.
C. He might not have gotten lost on his way here.
D. He may not have gotten lost on his way here.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menerka/berspekulasi alasan kejadian masa lalu menggunakan Modals. "Dia mungkin/bisa saja tersesat" direpresentasikan dengan "could have gotten". Menjawab ketidakhadirannya.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **He** | Verb: *could have gotten*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Modal for Speculation):** Subject + could/might/may + have + Verb 3

3. *Is* **that** the hospital where your brother had an operation?
A. No, he's not.
B. Yes, where's it?
C. That's a school.
**D. Yes, that's it.**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Merespon pertanyaan penunjuk tempat / referensi "Is that the hospital?" diiyakan dengan natural "Yes, that's it" (Ya, itu dia (tempatnya)).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **that** | Verb: *Is*, *is ('s)*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer for Designation):** Yes, that is it. / No, it is not.

4. *Is* **this** the coffee shop that has free wifi?
A. No, the coffee isn’t free.
B. No, it has delicious pastries.
**C. Yes, it is.**
D. Yes, the woman is talking loudly on her cell phone.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Jawaban paling tepat dan langsung untuk mengonfirmasi Subjek benda ("this") adalah dengan kata ganti "it". 
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **this**, **it** | Verb: *Is*, *is*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Verb To Be):** Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.

5. *Is* **the Netherlands**, which has many provinces at or below sea level, in danger of flooding?
**A. Yes, it is.**
B. Yes, because.
C. Since sea level is rising.
D. No, it is going down.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Walaupun kalimatnya panjang karena disisipi klausa relatif (which has many provinces...), inti kalimat tetap "Is the Netherlands ... in danger?". Kita balas singkat mengkonfirmasi keadaan negaranya.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **the Netherlands/it** | Verb: *Is*, *is*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Present Verb To Be):** Yes, Subject Pronoun + is/am/are.
