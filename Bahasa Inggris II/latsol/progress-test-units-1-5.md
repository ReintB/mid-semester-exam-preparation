# Materi Pembahasan Progress Test: Units 1-5

*Keterangan Format:*
- **Subject** pada soal ditandai dengan teks tebal (**bold**).
- *Verb* (Kata Kerja) pada kalimat ditandai dengan teks miring (*italic*).

## Vocabulary

1. *Have* **you** _____ the living room yet?
A. put away
**B. straightened up**
C. washed
D. cleaned out
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Pilihan "straightened up" adalah phrasal verb yang berarti merapikan ruangan agar barang-barang berada pada tempatnya dan terlihat teratur. 
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you** | Verb: *Have ... straightened up*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Tense Interrogative):** Have/Has + Subject + Verb 3

2. The brown belt karate **students** *have been* _____ for a competition for six months.
A. working
B. waiting
**C. preparing**
D. dressing
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Untuk menghadapi sebuah kompetisi (competition), tindakan yang paling masuk akal adalah melakukan persiapan ("preparing"). 
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **students** | Verb: *have been preparing*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Continuous Tense):** Subject + have/has + been + Verb-ing + for/since + Time

3. **Louisa** *has prepared* _____ for her family.
**A. dinner**
B. dishes
C. laundry
D. lawn
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kata kerja "prepared" (menyiapkan) paling umum dipasangkan dengan makanan, yaitu makan malam (dinner).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Louisa** | Verb: *has prepared*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Tense):** Subject + has/have + Verb 3

4. My classmate's **stomach** always *_____ (growls)* in class.
A. covers
**B. growls**
C. groans
D. moans
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Growls" merujuk pada suara keroncongan yang dikeluarkan oleh perut ketika seseorang merasa lapar.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **stomach** | Verb: *growls*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Present Tense):** Subject (Singular) + Verb 1 (-s/-es)

5. **She** *will be* satisfied with her career as a painter, but **she** *won't be* a _____ artist.
A. healthy
B. famous
**C. wealthy**
D. infamous
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menunjukkan kontras. Dia akan merasa puas dengan karir (kepuasan batin), namun tidak berarti dia akan kaya secara finansial ("wealthy").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **She** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *will be* (Klausa 1), *won't be* (Klausa 2)
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Future Tense - Nominal):** Subject + will + be + Adjective/Noun

6. An ancient **ruin** *was* _____ in the city of Plovdiv, Bulgaria.
A. sculpted
B. invented
C. designed
**D. discovered**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Reruntuhan kuno (ancient ruin) adalah sesuatu yang sudah ada sejak lama, sehingga kata kerjanya adalah ditemukan ("discovered").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **ruin** | Verb: *was discovered*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Past Passive):** Subject + was/were + Verb 3

7. **She** *is* not a safe driver because **she** *is* always ____ in her car.
A. tripping and falling
B. falling out
**C. singing and dancing**
D. driving
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Aktivitas yang mengalihkan perhatian (distraksi) saat menyetir. "Singing and dancing" adalah distraksi yang logis saat di dalam mobil.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **She** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *is* (Klausa 1), *is singing and dancing* (Klausa 2)
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Continuous Tense):** Subject + is/am/are + always + Verb-ing (menunjukkan kebiasaan yang terjadi terus)

8. The new **employee** *has been working* on a _____ for three days.
A. voicemail
**B. report**
C. on hold
D. competition
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Laporan ("report") adalah tugas profesional yang logis memakan waktu beberapa hari.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **employee** | Verb: *has been working*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Continuous Tense):** Subject + has/have + been + Verb-ing

9. Some famous **people** *are* not ____. **They** *are* well-known for another reason.
**A. wealthy**
B. satisfied
C. boss
D. fans
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kalimat ini menjelaskan bahwa menjadi terkenal tidak selalu menjamin seseorang kaya raya (wealthy).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **people**, **They** | Verb: *are*, *are*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Present - Nominal):** Subject + is/am/are + Adjective

10. **Kima** *has been* _____ the house all day.
A. sitting
B. leaving
**C. cleaning**
D. cooking
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Membersihkan rumah ("cleaning the house") adalah aktivitas harian yang umum dilakukan sepanjang hari untuk merawat tempat tinggal.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Kima** | Verb: *has been cleaning*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Continuous Tense):** Subject + has/have + been + Verb-ing

## Grammar

1. The **dishes** *____ (haven't been)* put away yet.
A. have been
B. had been
C. haven't
**D. haven't been**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kalimat ini menggunakan bentuk pasif dengan kata penanda "yet" (umumnya menuntut kalimat negatif). Karena "dishes" jamak, maka digunakan "have not".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **dishes** | Verb: *haven't been put*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Passive - Negative):** Subject + have/has + not + been + Verb 3

2. **Class** *begins* at 2 o'clock and _____ at 2:50.
A. end
B. ended
**C. ends**
D. ending
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Simple Present digunakan untuk jadwal atau rutinitas tetap. Subjek "Class" (tunggal) membutuhkan penambahan akhiran -s/-es ("begins" & "ends").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Class** | Verb: *begins*, *ends*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Present Tense):** Subject (Singular) + Verb 1 (-s/-es)

3. The **monument** *____ (was built)* to remember the country's most famous writer.
A. is built
**B. was built**
C. building
D. built
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Monumen dibangun di masa lampau (tindakan yang sudah selesai), sehingga menggunakan bentuk Simple Past Passive.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **monument** | Verb: *was built*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Past Passive):** Subject + was/were + Verb 3

4. The **students** *were talking* in class when the **teacher** *____ (told)* them to stop.
**A. told**
B. was telling
C. tells
D. is telling
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung (were talking) disela oleh peristiwa baru yang lebih singkat (told). Kata hubung "when" selalu diikuti aksi penyela dalam bentuk past tense.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **students**, **teacher** | Verb: *were talking*, *told*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Continuous interrupted by Simple Past):** Subject + was/were + Verb-ing + *when* + Subject + Verb 2

5. *Is* **Elle** *____ (going to be)* satified with her career?
A. goes
**B. going to be**
C. gone to be
D. go being
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Mengomunikasikan prediksi atau rencana masa depan menggunakan struktur "be going to". Karena "satisfied" adalah kata sifat (Adjective), maka butuh "be" sebelum kata sifat tersebut.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Elle** | Verb: *Is ... going to be*
> **Rumus / Pola (Future - Be going to Interrogative):** Is/Am/Are + Subject + going to + be + Adjective

6. It's 3 o'clock and my **brother** *_____ (hasn't)* washed the car yet.
A. hadn't
**B. hasn't**
C. haven't
D. didn't
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Adanya pertanda waktu "yet" mengindikasikan tense Present Perfect. "Brother" merupakan subjek tunggal sehingga menggunakan "hasn't" sebagai kata bantu.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **brother** | Verb: *hasn't washed*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Tense - Negative):** Subject + has/have + not + Verb 3

7. The **orchestra** *has been _____ (practicing)* for a special concert.
A. practice
B. practices
**C. practicing**
D. practiced
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Aksi dimulai di waktu lampau tapi masih terus berlangsung hingga momen saat diutarakan, ditandani dengan kata bantu "has been" yang menuntut V-ing.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **orchestra** | Verb: *has been practicing*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Continuous Tense):** Subject + has/have + been + Verb-ing

8. **Rita** *____ (was sleeping)* when her **roommates** *came* home from the movies.
A. sleeps
B. slept
C. is sleeping
**D. was sleeping**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Sama seperti soal nomor 4, kejadian yang panjang berlangsung (sedang tidur) lalu dipotong oleh kejadian lain (teman datang).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Rita**, **roommates** | Verb: *was sleeping*, *came*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Continuous interrupted by Simple Past):** Subject + was/were + Verb-ing + *when* + Subject + Verb 2

9. **Skyscrapers** *_____ (are located)* in big cities like New York.
**A. are located**
B. located
C. location
D. was located
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menyampaikan fakta umum tentang di mana benda berada menggunakan pola pasif masa kini (Passive Present). "Skyscrapers" jamak sehingga menggunakan "are".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Skyscrapers** | Verb: *are located*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Present Passive):** Subject + is/am/are + Verb 3

10. **Nick** *_____ (has been)* searching for his phone all afternoon.
A. have been
**B. has been**
C. was been
D. is been
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kata kunci "all afternoon" menunjukkan durasi yang berlangsung hingga sekarang, jadi digunakan Perfect Continuous. Nick adalah subjek tunggal.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Nick** | Verb: *has been searching*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Continuous Tense):** Subject + has/have + been + Verb-ing

## Language and Use

1. *Has* the **laundry** *been folded* yet?
**A. No, it hasn't.**
B. No, it has been.
C. No, it didn't.
D. No, it wasn't.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Jawaban singkat (Short Answer) harus selaras dengan kata tanya awalan kalimat. Pertanyaan diawali "Has", jawaban harus sejalan: Has -> has/hasn't.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **laundry/it** | Verb: *Has been folded*, *hasn't*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Present Perfect):** Yes, Subject Pronoun + has/have. / No, Subject Pronoun + hasn't/haven't.

2. *Will* your **boss** *let* you go on vacation?
A. Let's go on vacation.
B. No, she doesn't.
C. No, she willn't.
**D. No, she won't.**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Pertanyaan ini bermula dari "Will", dan bentuk singkatan (contraction) negatif yang benar dari "Will not" adalah "won't".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **boss/she** | Verb: *Will let*, *won't*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Simple Future):** Yes, Subject Pronoun + will. / No, Subject Pronoun + won't.

3. *Are* **you** *going to study* for the test tomorrow?
**A. Yes, I am.**
B. Yes, I study.
C. Yes, I am studying.
D. Yes, I'm going now.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Pertanyaan menggunakan be "Are" (ditujukan untuk subjek "you"). Maka jawaban ditujukan ke diri sendiri sehingga mengubah subjek jadi "I" dan verb-to-be berubah jadi "am".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you/I** | Verb: *Are going to study*, *am*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Be Going to):** Yes, Subject Pronoun + is/am/are.

4. *Have* **you** *made* your bed?
A. No, I hadn't.
B. No, you hadn't.
**C. Yes, I have.**
D. Yes, I've.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Jawaban singkat bentuk positif tidak boleh diucapkan dengan cara disingkat. "I've" tidak boleh berdiri sendiri sebagai bagian akhir struktur short answer, harus dijawab penuh "I have".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you/I** | Verb: *Have made*, *have*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Present Perfect):** Yes, Subject Pronoun + have/has.

5. *Was* this **building** *designed* by Gaudi?
A. Yes, it is.
**B. Yes, it was.**
C. Yes, he was.
D. It was in Barcelona.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Building" merupakan benda perwakilan Pronoun "it". Pertanyaan masa lalu (past) dimulai "Was", jadi dijawab menggunakan "was" kembali.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **building/it** | Verb: *Was designed*, *was*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Simple Past Passive):** Yes, Subject Pronoun + was/were.
