- *Verb* (Kata Kerja) pada kalimat ditandai dengan teks miring (*italic*).

## Vocabulary

1. If **we** *____ (advertise)* on social media, **we** *could reach* so many more people.
A. put together
B. put up
C. rent
**D. advertise**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Beriklan (advertise) di media sosial adalah cara yang masuk akal secara konteks untuk menjangkau (reach) lebih banyak audiens.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **we** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *advertise* (Klausa 1), *could reach* (Klausa 2)
> **Rumus / Pola (Second Conditional Modal):** If + Subject + Verb 1, Subject + could + Verb 1

2. The **manager** *said*, "*____ (Arrange)* for a meeting room for our team from noon to 5 pm."
A. Change
B. Take
C. Use
**D. Arrange**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Arrange" berarti mengatur atau menyiapkan pemesanan. Kata ini sangat lazim disandingkan dengan persiapan tempat pertemuan (meeting room).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **manager**, (Implied You/Someone) | Verb: *said*, *Arrange*
> **Rumus / Pola (Direct Speech - Imperative):** Subject + said, "Verb 1 + Object"

3. After **I** *graduated* from college **I** *____ (applied)* for employment at several companies.
A. accepted
**B. applied**
C. relocated
D. offered
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Seseorang lazimnya melamar (applied) untuk sebuah pekerjaan (employment) sehabis lulus kuliah. 
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **I** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *graduated*, *applied*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Past with Time Clause):** After + Subject + Verb 2, Subject + Verb 2

4. My **boss** *told* me, "**I**'*ll meet* you outside the ____ at 10 o'clock."
**A. convention center**
B. business trip
C. conference call
D. courier
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Outside the..." menuntut penjelasan lokasi fisik bangunan. "Convention center" (gedung konferensi/pameran) adalah satu-satunya pilihan yang merupakan lokasi tempat fisik.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **boss**, **I** | Verb: *told*, *will meet ('ll meet)*
> **Rumus / Pola (Direct Speech - Future Tense):** Subject + will + Verb 1

5. Although **I** *am* ____, **I** *have to travel* by plane for my job.
A. using an online dating site
B. taking a language course abroad
**C. afraid of heights**
D. acting in a play
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Adanya kata "Although" menunjukkan pertentangan. Takut ketinggian ("afraid of heights") sangat bertentangan dan menjadi tantangan jika tuntutan pekerjaan adalah naik pesawat ("travel by plane").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **I** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *am*, *have to travel*
> **Rumus / Pola (Concession Clause):** Although + Subject + is/am/are + Adjective, Subject + have/has to + Verb 1

6. If **she** *had eaten* lunch, **she** *wouldn't be* so ____ now.
**A. hungry**
B. sleepy
C. worried
D. embarrassed
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menunjukkan penyesalan/kondisi yang membayangkan masa lalu memengaruhi masa sekarang. Jika tadi dia makan, sekarang dia tidak akan lapar ("hungry").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **she** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *had eaten*, *wouldn't be*
> **Rumus / Pola (Mixed Conditional):** If + Subject + had + Verb 3 (Past), Subject + would/wouldn't + be + Adjective (Present)

7. Before **I** *got* a job, **I** *had* an ____ at an engineering company.
A. employment
**B. internship**
C. relocation
D. corporation
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Magang (internship) adalah tahap perolehan pengalaman logis sebelum seseorang mendapatkan pekerjaan tetap ("got a job").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **I** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *got*, *had*
> **Rumus / Pola (Simple Past Tense):** Before + Subject + Verb 2, Subject + Verb 2

8. Even though **she** *has* a lot of friends, **Martine** sometimes *____ (goes to the movies)* alone.
A. runs a marathon
B. acts in a play
C. gives a speech
**D. goes to the movies**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "Even though" menunjukkan kontras. Kontras dengan "punya banyak teman" adalah melakukan aktivitas hiburan sendirian, dalam hal ini pergi ke bioskop (goes to the movies).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **she**, **Martine** | Verb: *has*, *goes*
> **Rumus / Pola (Concession - Simple Present):** Even though + Subject + Verb 1 (-s/-es), Subject + Adverb of Freq + Verb 1 (-s/-es)

9. If **we** *don't hire* muscians the **____ (fundraiser)** *won't have* an enjoyable atmosphere.
A. to-do list
B. volunteers
**C. fundraiser**
D. donations
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Acara yang disebutkan di sini dan membutuhkan "enjoyable atmosphere" (suasana menyenangkan) dengan pemusik adalah "fundraiser" (acara penggalangan dana).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **we**, **fundraiser** | Verb: *don't hire*, *won't have*
> **Rumus / Pola (First Conditional):** If + Subject + do/does not + Verb 1, Subject + will not (won't) + Verb 1

10. **I** *wouldn't have been* so ____ if **they** *hadn't laughed* at me.
A. thirsty
**B. embarrassed**
C. sleepy
D. tired
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Ditertawakan keliru sangat lazim menimbulkan perasaan malu ("embarrassed").
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **I**, **they** | Verb: *wouldn't have been*, *hadn't laughed*
> **Rumus / Pola (Third Conditional):** Subject + would not + have + been + Adjective + if + Subject + had not + Verb 3

## Grammar

1. My **boss** *____ (told)* me, "You can order room service or have dinner in a restaurant."
A. said
**B. told**
C. asked
D. explained
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kata kerja "tell/told" harus diikuti tepat dengan objek orang (dalam hal ini "me"). Berbeda dengan "said" yang tidak bisa langsung diakhiri "me" tanpa kata "to".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **boss** | Verb: *told*
> **Rumus / Pola (Reporting Verbs):** Subject + told + Object (Person)

2. *____ (While)* **Enrique** *has* many girlfriends, his **brother Julio** *doesn't* so he uses an online dating site.
A. When
B. Meanwhile
**C. While**
D. Because
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "While" dalam konteks ini berfungsi sebagai kata penghubung (conjunction) yang digunakan untuk membandingkan dua situasi kontras secara bersamaan (Satu banyak pacar, satu jomblo).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Enrique**, **brother Julio** | Verb: *has*, *doesn't (have)*
> **Rumus / Pola (Conjunction of Contrast):** While + Subject 1 + Verb, Subject 2 + Verb + (Negative)

3. "Where is the convention center?" the **intern** *____ (asked me)* .
A. me asked
**B. asked me**
C. reported
D. explained to me
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Dalam melaporkan kalimat tanya, susunan yang baku adalah Subjek Penanya ("intern") lalu Kata Kerja Pelapor ("asked") diikuti Objek yang ditanya ("me"). 
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **intern** | Verb: *asked*
> **Rumus / Pola (Quoted Speech Subject/Verb position):** "..." Subject + Reporting Verb + Object

4. *____ (While)* **I** *was* in college **I** *worked* part-time in the library.
**A. While**
B. Whenever
C. Since
D. During
> **Penjelasan Detail:** "While" berarti "Ketika/Selama" dan harus diikuti oleh sebuah klausa berstruktur penuh (S + V). Berbeda dengan "During" yang hanya diikuti noun phrase.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **I** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *was*, *worked*
> **Rumus / Pola (Time Conjunction - Past):** While + Subject + was/were + Complement, Subject + Verb 2

5. If **we** *organize* volunteers **we** *_____ (won't)* have to hire people to work at the event.
A. will
B. could
**C. won't**
D. couldn't
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kalimat pengandaian Tipe 1 (First Conditional) berisi konsekuensi logis yang bernada negatif: Jika ada relawan, kita jadi "tidak perlu (won't)" mempekerjakan/membayar orang.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **we** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *organize*, *won't have to hire*
> **Rumus / Pola (First Conditional Negative Result):** If + Subject + Verb 1, Subject + will not (won't) + Verb 1

6. *____ (Even though)* **Teresa** *is* shy, **she** *gives* great speeches to large audiences at conferences.
A. When
**B. Even though**
C. While
D. Since
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Menunjukkan pertentangan tegas yang mengejutkan (Pemalu tapi jago pidato). "Even though" merupakan conjunction yang paling tepat untuk fakta yang kontras ini.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **Teresa**, **she** | Verb: *is*, *gives*
> **Rumus / Pola (Conjunction of Concession):** Even though + Subject + Verb/Adjective, Subject + Verb

7. **They** *would've felt* better if **they** *hadn't ____ (eaten)* so much salty food.
**A. eaten**
B. eat
C. ate
D. to eat
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Kalimat Pengandaian Tipe 3 (Third Conditional). Syarat klausa "if" harus berupa Past Perfect berselimut "had/hadn't" yang diposisikan sebelum Verb 3. Verb 3 dari eat adalah "eaten".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **They** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *would've felt*, *hadn't eaten*
> **Rumus / Pola (Third Conditional - If Clause):** If + Subject + had not + Verb 3

8. *____ (Since)* **I** *was* young, **I**'*ve always wanted* to study abroad.
A. While
B. When
C. Before
**D. Since**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Frase "have always wanted" menandakan Present Perfect (kegiatan dari masa lalu sampai sekarang), pasangan awalan periode waktunya yang ideal adalah "Since", artinya Sejak masa lalu sampai saat ini.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **I** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *was*, *'ve (have) wanted*
> **Rumus / Pola (Present Perfect Time Signal):** Since + Subject + was/were + Adjective, Subject + have/has + Verb 3

9. If **we** *held* an auction, **we** *____ (could)* raise money for a charity.
A. will
B. won't
**C. could**
D. couldn't
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Pengandaian Tipe 2 (Second Conditional) menggunakan kata kerja bentuk lampau ("held") pada klausa If, sehingga di klausa utama membutuhkan modal past seperti "could" atau "would".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **we** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *held*, *could raise*
> **Rumus / Pola (Second Conditional):** If + Subject + Verb 2, Subject + would/could + Verb 1

10. **She** *would've been* less stressed if **she** *____ (had practiced)* her presentation before she had to present it to the class.
A. was practicing
B. practiced
C. did practice
**D. had practiced**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Sama seperti nomor 7, klausa hasil menggunakan "would've been" (Tipe 3). Karenanya, bagian di belakang "If (...)" wajib menggunakan Past Perfect (had + Verb 3 / had practiced).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **She** (Klausa 1 & 2) | Verb: *would've been*, *had practiced*
> **Rumus / Pola (Third Conditional):** Subject + would have + Verb 3 + if + Subject + had + Verb 3

## Language and Use

1. Even though you speak English well, *are* **you** afraid to give a speech in English?
**A. No, I'm not.**
B. No, I don't.
C. No, I didn't.
D. No, I'm giving it to you.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Untuk pertanyaan present status "Are you...?", jawabannya harus sinkron dengan subjek To Be: yes I am / No I'm not.
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you / I** | Verb: *are*, *'m not (am not)*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Present Be):** Yes, I am. / No, I am not.

2. If you could make a donation to the auction, what *would* **you** *give*?
A. I could give something.
B. I couldn't give.
**C. I would give some books.**
D. I would given a gift certificate.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Pertanyaan meminta objek spesifik dengan struktur "what would you give?". Tanggapan standar membalas pola pengandaian tersebut dengan Subjek + Would + Verb 1 + (Barangnya/Sesuatu).
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you / I** | Verb: *would give*, *would give*
> **Rumus / Pola (Responding to Second Conditional Question):** Subject + would + Verb 1 + Object

3. What *did* **you** *want* to be when you were young?
A. I want to be a journalist.
**B. I wanted to be a journalist.**
C. I have wanted to be a journalist.
D. While I was a young journalist.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Pertanyaan tertuju pada ambisi masa lampau dengan bentuk "did ... want". Untuk menjawab, kata kerja diubah seketika ke bentuk Simple Past (Verb 2) yakni "wanted".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you / I** | Verb: *did want*, *wanted*
> **Rumus / Pola (Past Tense Statement Response):** Subject + Verb 2 + Object

4. *Would* **you** *have been* upset if I hadn't called you from New York?
A. Yes, I was upset.
B. Yes, I have been upset.
C. No, I haven't been upset.
**D. Yes, I would have been upset.**
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Ini adalah struktur pengandaian Tipe 3 pada pertanyaan. Menjawabnya secara utuh harus mengulang pola Tense yang sama yakni "Would have been".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **you / I** | Verb: *Would have been*, *would have been*
> **Rumus / Pola (Third Conditional Affirmative Response):** Yes, Subject + would have been + Adjective

5. *Did* **she** *say*, "take the clients out to dinner"?
**A. No, she didn't.**
B. No, we had lunch.
C. No, I took the clients to dinner.
D. No, the clients have left.
> **Penjelasan Detail:** Jawaban negatif singkat (Short Answer) terhadap kalimat tanya Simple Past "Did she...?". Sangat sederhana, cukup pasangkan pronoun "she" dengan "did not (didn't)".
> **Analisis Kalimat:** Subject: **she** | Verb: *Did say*, *didn't*
> **Rumus / Pola (Short Answer Simple Past):** Yes, Subject + did. / No, Subject + didn't.
